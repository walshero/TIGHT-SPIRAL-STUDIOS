## 1. THE FOUR-FIFTHS THESIS ⭐ *never captured until now*
**Yours. Named by you. This session.**

> *"I really felt moved by Atlas Shrugged and Fountainhead, but by the time I got to
> the Virtue of Selfishness, I realized the intellectual trick she played better than
> anyone I'd read yet. I called it the 4/5ths thesis. My own idea."*

**The idea:** carry the reader through four-fifths of a decent argument, earn their
trust, and then at the pivot swap the premise for its opposite while they're still
nodding.

**Why it matters beyond the essay:** this is a *general-purpose rhetorical diagnostic*.
It is not a Rand observation or a Vance observation — it's a reusable tool for reading
any argument. It could be a game. It could be a Writerly Move. It is currently
one paragraph in one essay draft.

**SEED NEEDING WATER:** this is the most portable idea in the archive and it has no home.

## 2. PROVENANCE IS THE CONTENT ⭐ *parked, never built*
**From: "Mapping ideas through human lineage" (2026-06-24)**

**The idea:** map the lineage of ideas — not the ideas, but *the people, discourse
communities, and teachable moments* that shaped them. Where and from whom knowledge
was learned is **not metadata. It is the primary unit of meaning.**

The unit is **"the encounter"** — person-to-person-through-idea, not concept-to-concept.

infrastructure of *lived, attributed human knowledge* — explicitly positioned as the
**alternative to scraped, de-attributed data.**

# PART II — YOUR RULINGS (stated once, in a chat, never folded into law)

## Design rulings

**"This is not gamification — learning science drives the instructional design."**
Stated explicitly and repeatedly. This is the studio's founding distinction and it
lives in a chat summary.

**"Start small. Lock the right thing good and small first, then magnify."**
Specifically: *lock the cards before any mechanics discussion.* A sequencing law.

**"The rhetorical situation determines the medium and the visual approach — never
SVG by habit."** LOCKED principle. Currently violated by default in most builds.

**The four asset rules (C1–C4), locked 2026-06-25:**
- **C1** — joy and novelty in **every** asset
- **C2** — the entry point is **huge** as a rule
- **C3** — palette **always deliberately composed**
- **C4** — cognitive science grounds **all** aspects

**The Singular/Plural card taxonomy:**
- **Singular** (only one per piece): First Domino, Start After the Storm
- **Plural** (stackable, many per piece): World Detail That Matters, Interior Weather,
  Backstory Snap, Slide

**"Students apply cards to a neutral draft *I* write"** — not their own work, not
AI-generated text. A pedagogical ruling with real teeth.

**Dignity-first floor (§3.1):** every build treats the person as whole, capable, and
the author of their own situation. Ask don't assume. **A starting point is never a
verdict.** Consequences land on circumstance, not worth. **Resources are framed as
assets, not deficits — money and struggle language are NEVER on screen one.**

**Friction reduction by design** — a standing principle. Setup, delivery, and pipeline
should minimize handoffs wherever tooling allows.
