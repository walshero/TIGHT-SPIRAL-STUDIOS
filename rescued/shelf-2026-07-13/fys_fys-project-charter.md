## Why this is its own lane
FYS has been running, in some form, since the 2010 pilot Matt built (embedding FYS outcomes into the writing sequence: LN090/EN090, WR100/EN100, EN101). It has its own outcomes, its own faculty pool, its own calendar rhythm, its own evidence base, and — as of the June 16, 2026 retreat — its own expansion agenda. It deserves a container that does not get lost inside general MassBay committee work.

## The living learning outcome
> Students will demonstrate the ability to use the wealth of academic, cultural, and social resources at MassBay for self-advocacy and individual empowerment.

Supporting strands: successful **transition** (responsibility + real community membership), **self-advocacy**, **belonging** (critical on a commuter campus), and **academic practice** (critical reading, entering the academic conversation with integrity, research as habit).
