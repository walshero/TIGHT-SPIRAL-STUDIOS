*Run by three audit tendrils under the founder's no-HITL grant. Read-only: no game was edited in the audit. Arithmetic gate (one-thing-gate.py, entry paint + nav tooth) plus source review for dark-mode, emoji, scene-first.*

## THE CURRENT PRINCIPLE SET (what every build is now measured against)
1. Scene-first — open on a scene, never a wall of text.
2. One invitation — exactly one primary call-to-act on entry.
3. Majority image — entry tableau >=50% image.
4. **Home + Back on every screen** (new, 2026-07-13). *(gate tooth added)*
5. **Dark-mode-proof** — legible in a dark AND a light comfort stop (new, 2026-07-13). *(studio-eyes lane)*
6. No emoji, ever. Comfort is a corner knob, never a gate.

## SYSTEMIC FINDINGS (the patterns, not the one-offs)
- **NAV FLOOR UNMET SUITE-WIDE.** 0 of 15 audited builds expose a Home control. Only `confluence-console` has a true Back. This is the single biggest gap and it is now an enforced tooth. The flagship (`cliche-field-v6`) is the reference fix.
- **DARK-MODE LOCKS.** Dark-locked (no light stop): `cliche-line`, `cliche-city`, `cliche-cabinet`. Light-locked (no dark stop): `convergence-card-engine`, `choose-your-leader-full`. The rest carry three real stops.
- **COMFORT-GATE-AS-WALL** — a named failure class as of today. `flash-ballast` and `review-bench` OPEN on a comfort-palette selection screen. That is the "wall before the scene" anti-pattern wearing an accessibility costume. This is what "the review bench is gated" means — no lock, a comfort wall. Fix: palette moves to the corner knob; open scene-first.
- **CO-EQUAL INVITATIONS.** Two primaries on entry: `funny-boneys-factory`, `convergence-card-engine`, `sandbags-joy`, `behind-this-door`, `choose-your-leader-full`, `en195-hub`.
- **UNDER-SIZED SCENE.** Opens scene-first but the visual is below 50%: the-bridge 9%, sandbags-joy 11%, behind-this-door 13%, confluence 32%, funny-boneys 43%.
- **EMOJI FLOOR HOLDS.** Zero emoji anywhere in the 15. That floor is working.
