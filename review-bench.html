The bench

You're the reviewer today.

A classmate hands you a flash piece. The hardest part of workshop isn't being nice or being harsh — it's naming what you see.

So you won't write loose comments. You'll read the piece line by line and place a named move on the lines that earn one — the same moves from the Workshop Wall. Then you'll see what a strong reviewer marked, and where you agreed.

The rule of the bench: a review is about the piece, never the writer. You're not grading a person. You're pointing at what the words are doing — and what one small move could do next.

This piece is a sample we wrote for practice — not anyone's real work. You warm up here, then bring these names to your real classmates.
