How this plays

Five beats, on a loop. The rail up top shows where you are.

1 · BuildGuess which beat is funny. The guess is the game.
2 · ShowPut it in front of a crowd you choose.
3 · PollAsk them — and how you ask changes the truth.
4 · See the gapYour guess vs. what actually landed. A number.
5 · RecalibrateUse the gap to guess better next time.

A flat gag is never a failure here. It is the crowd telling you something you didn't know. That is worth more than an easy laugh.
