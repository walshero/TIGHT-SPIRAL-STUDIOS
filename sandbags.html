OVERWRITTEN BALLOON CO.

A balloon sits on the ground. It is roped to the dirt by every extra word in the draft tied to it.

It wants to fly. It can't. It's carrying too much.

You're going to make it fly.

Not by adding anything. By cutting weight.

Tap any word in the draft to drop it. Each word you cut drops as a sandbag — and the balloon rises. When the piece is light enough, it lifts off. That's the whole game.

One promise: a word you cut isn't a bad word. It's just weight this flight doesn't need. It can fly another day, in another piece.
