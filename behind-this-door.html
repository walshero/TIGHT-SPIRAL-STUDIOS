How this was made

Every door here is a cut-paper interpretation built in code — layered SVG shapes, not photographs. The details you read as clues (a fanlight, a porthole, a striped crossing) are mnemonic design: each door remembers its world for you.

Facts are held to verifiable general knowledge about each artist and place; where a door's exact paint color isn't certain, the art interprets rather than claims. Built by Claude in the Tight Spiral production pipeline; the founder's eyes are the final gate on every visual call.
