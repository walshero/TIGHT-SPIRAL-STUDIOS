The Funnyboney Factory

Where a serious command is forged by a deeply unserious machine.

You coin a short, daft, memorable word. You tell it what to do. Then a ridiculous contraption "runs" it — and because the contraption is unforgettable, so is the word. Out the far end drops your real command, ready to paste into any chatbot, or wire to a Shortcut later. The joke is the memory trick.

Forge a spell

A word your mouth enjoys. Absurd beats sensible — you'll remember KAZOO long after you've forgotten "run_sweep_v2."

This is what KAZOO actually means. Write it once; never type it again.

Press "Run it through the machine" and watch your word earn its keep.

  1. The drop. Your token rolls down the ramp, full of false confidence.
  2. The quill. It bonks a quill, which thinks it's being asked to write.
  3. The spill. The quill knocks the inkpot, which spills with great drama.
  4. The bell. The splash rings a bell that has waited its whole life for this.
  5. The cannon. The bell trips the scroll cannon — and out flies your actual command.
