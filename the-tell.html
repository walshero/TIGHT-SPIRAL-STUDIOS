The reading room

Somebody read this before you.

They tagged a moment in the piece — named the move they saw working, or the thing it needs. Their tag is face-down. You don't get to see it yet.

Your job: read, tap a tagged spot, call the move and say why — your evidence. Then flip, and see what the last reader called.

The point isn't a right answer. Two good readers can tag the same line differently and both be right. The flip shows you a defensible read next to yours — and asks whose evidence is stronger.
