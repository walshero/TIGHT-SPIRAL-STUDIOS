## WHY THE DIAGNOSE MODE MATTERS

It is the most pedagogically sophisticated thing in the whole repo. It does not quiz
definitions. It takes a **revision problem you actually have** —

> *"My draft explains every movement between scenes and the energy keeps leaking out."*
> → **Sliding**
> *"The plot has events, but the character's choices feel random."*
> → **Misbelief Engine**

— and routes you to the move that fixes it. **That is transfer, not recall.** Every
other game in the studio is reaching for what this already does.
