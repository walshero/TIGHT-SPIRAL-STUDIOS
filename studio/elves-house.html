The Elves' House

The elves came while you slept

Each room is rigged with a different toy. All of them are yours. All of them hold the floor.

Walk through. When a mechanism sparks something — when you think that one, do that everywhere — press its "This one — spread it" button. At the bottom, the house tallies what you lit, so you can hand the joy back to the system.
