What this proved

The harvest: Lane A's ceiling is high enough that Lane B becomes an on-call contractor, not infrastructure. Craft moves demonstrated in one file: edge shadows selling the glue, matte grain instead of gradients, real value range with temperature relief, per-scene lighting with two identifiable sources, gravity honored on every bench object, and detail that suggests instead of simulates - three scraps read as a working bench; thirty would read as noise.
