homes on different clocks, through different interpreters, to different children.

## DOMAIN REP — the three diets are documented distribution, not opinion
| Home | Channel | Clock | Interpreter |
|---|---|---|---|
| Suburban | Live network TV | 7 PM, same night | The networks |
| City apartment | Chicago Defender, Jet | Weekly | A Black press covering what the networks did not |
| Rural | Weekly paper; radio if reached | Days late | Local editor |

The instructive fact: the rural child and the city child are NOT less informed.
They are differently informed, on different clocks, by different interpreters.

## STORYBOARD
- **First screen:** a room. No menu. No preference wall. Scene first.
- **The stamp:** the knock — knock the phone off the hook, the adults' conversation
  becomes audible.
- **Loop:** move, overhear, MISS. The misses are the content.
- **Record:** turns in batch, on everything.
- **Exit / mirror:** replays the player's own path.
  "You never went where the radio was" is the entire media-literacy read.
  NO LECTURE. The telemetry is the confession.

stood somewhere else. The Provenance thesis, in a house.
